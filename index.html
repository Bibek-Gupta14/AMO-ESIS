Employees' State Insurance Scheme (ESIS), Meghalaya

The Employees' State Insurance Scheme (ESIS) was implemented from September 1980 in Shillong area and extended to Byrnihat in May 2005.

The ESIS is a multi-dimensional Social Security Scheme for the Welfare of the Labour Class and Weaker Section of Society. All those under coverage are entitled to two types of benefits:

Benefits

1.

Cash Benefit

  • Sickness Benefit
  • Extended Sick Benefit
  • Enhanced Sickness Benefit
  • Maternity Benefit
  • Dependent Benefit
  • Disablement Benefit
  • Funeral Benefit
2.

Medical Benefit

Comprehensive medical care for the insured persons and their family members.

  • Medical treatment for IP and family
  • Retired & permanently disabled IPs get medical care
  • Cashless treatment at empanelled hospitals
  • No ceiling on medical expenditure
  • Coverage for secondary/speciality care
